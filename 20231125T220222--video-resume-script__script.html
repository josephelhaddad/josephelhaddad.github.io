1.1. Draft: Sales pitch component

1.1.1. The problem

  • Hiring process take a long time and there is no shortcut to find a good talent that can pivot and have learning agility.
  • Hiring the wrong employee will cost you Money, Time, Moral, and Productivity.
  • Employees retention.

1.1.2. The solution

  • Making new candid take some assessments to filter out some.
  • Having opportunity for growth

1.1.3. The value proposition (why me?)

  • My prove of pivoting and having a learning agility is me learning Statistics, Data analysis, and recently script writing and business hiring process.
  • As someone from a third world country I can make a good living white being payed less.

1.1.4. Draft

The hiring process is tedious, not only you are looking for a unicorn or a jack of all trades that can both pivot and have a leaning agility, but also avoiding big-egos that will cost your business Money, Time, Moral, and Productivity; on top of that, trying to retain them for the longest time.

Some businesses uses assessments like Enneagram and DICS test to check a candid personality, some business even requires a video presentation and a writing assessment.

I did all of this before being asked, I started as a data analysis, studied more statistics, data science, scripting and automation, video writing, and business hiring practice to understand the process.

I am, as the quote that states, a "Jack of all trades, master of none", I have both learning agility and the ability to pivot and help in any department in your business.

Furthermore, I also live in a Third World country, I can be paid less that what is considered average in First world country and still live a good life.
