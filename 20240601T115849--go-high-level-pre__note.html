1. In general

Small businesses owners turn to marketing agencies to help them get more buyers, not just leads.

1.1. Client treatment

How to keep your clients.

  • Be honest with them.
  • Own up to your mistakes.
  • Respond quickly.
  • Keep them updated.
  • Educational resources in a form of blogs or/and videos.
  • Revise your email and ads before sending/publishing.

1.2. Concept

The clients don't want you to hand them leads, and they want buyers.

  • Leads are potential buyers that you got their contact information from ads, forms, or manualy.
    How to get least will be answered below.
  • CRM service: tracking ROI by following the leads journey, that we got from multiple ad platform, and try to turn them into buyers using follow ups.
  • Follow ups: are actions executed if conditions are met, they are like automatic strategies in a form of flow chart, how are they created? Should we wait one five, or 10 minutes before re contacting someone?…? Knowledge and experience both help decide.

3. Go high level

  • Your account comes with a dashboard, you an create a subaccount for your client and give it to them, even as a SaaS.
  • Inside each subaccount you can:
    • Add your clients' personal information manually or via Google business map view; tho some stuff, like email, will still be needed to be entered manually because it cannot be found on Google business map.
    • Enable/disable SaaS features.
    • Pre-load some repetitive stuff using snapshots.
    • Add custom links in their dashboard, example:
      • Link to your Facebook group.
      • Link to meeting scheduling.
      • Link to live support.
      • …

3.1. How to capture leads?

  1. Forms:
    1. Websites, funnels, widgets, offers, and surveys.
      To create them, go to Dashboard \(\rightarrow\) Sites
    2. Online ad forms using platforms like Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, Google… Platforms should be first integrated to the account/subaccount.
      To integrate, go to Dashboard \(\rightarrow\) Integrations
  2. Direct messages.
    The messages can be found in Dashboard \(\rightarrow\) Conversations
    1. Social media DMs, require social media integration.
      To integrate, go to Dashboard \(\rightarrow\) Integrations
    2. Calls and texts, both require you to add a phone number, and integrate google and enable GMB call tracking.
      To add a phone number, go to Dashboard \(\rightarrow\) Settings \(\rightarrow\) Phone number
      To integrate google, go to Dashboard \(\rightarrow\) Integrations
      doing this can add a chat button in your client google business page in google map.
      Note: Most people that contact your client via phone are recurrent customers, not new lead that should be added to followups.
    3. Emails, need to be setup in.
      To set up an email, go to Dashboard \(\rightarrow\) Settings \(\rightarrow\) Email services
  3. Manual entry.
    1. Walks-in/in person conversation
      To add a lead information manually, go to Dashboard \(\rightarrow\) access quick action by clicking on green lightning bolt \(\rightarrow\) add a contact
    2. List upload … secret
      To upload a list, Dashboard \(\rightarrow\) Contacts \(\rightarrow\) Import contact
