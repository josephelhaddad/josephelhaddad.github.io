1. Intro

A friend of mine shared his interest in making trivia videos on YouTube, and because I love under promising and over delivering, I had decided to get him a dataset of Q&A for him to use in his "project".
Note that I gathered data for my friend once before and did not see anything come out of it.

2. Plan

After 10 minutes of searching on Google, which is a hell of a long time, I stumbled upon a website called Trivia Nerd; this website have the kids categories that he is planning to target, but I wasn't satisfied with the quantity.

And so I have sinned and searched on Reddit for a trivia website, and found this: j-archive, which is a website that archive Jeopardy's Q&A.
