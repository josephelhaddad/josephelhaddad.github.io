1.1. Get started with clickup

  • The sidebar in click up dashboard contain spaces and workspaces that are generated by your team lead.
  • You can declutter your sidebar by hiding spaces.
    To hide a space, hover over the space, click the three dots, and click "Leave Space", not "Delete space".
    To show a hidden space, click on "Show all spaces", chose a space and click "Join"

1.2. Create Your First Task

  • At the list level, a task can be created with a priority, due date, and an assignee or assignees.

1.3. Customize Your Task

  • Upon clicking on a task, there are things that can be added/changed, among them:
    • Task status can be updated.
    • Watcher, a person that get notified when a task is updated, can be added by clicking on the bell icon.
    • Task description and subtasks can be added.
    • Task relationship, which are links to other tasks, can be added.

1.4. Collaborate With Your Team

  • Collaborate using comment, tagging and assigning a teammate will let them receive a notification in their inbox.
    • Tagging: @ tag a teammate in your comment and expect a comment back., the person will be added as a watcher
    • Assigning: you assign a teammate to your comment and expect the concern/problem to be addressed/resolve
    • Inbox: all communications are placed in the inbox as notifications.

1.5. Manage Notification Settings

  • You can declutter your inbox by switching off what type of notifications will be shown and where they are sent.

1.6. Manage Your Work

  • views are modes that display task differently, you can add a custom view.
  • the "Me mode" filter out your teammates task in the views, allowing you to only see that tasks assigned to you.

1.7. Find Your Work

  • Favorites: mark a task as favorite, and you can find in on the left sidebar.
  • Search: you can use the search bar to look for a task.
