Hi Joseph,

Good work on the email and trying this. So I thought I'd give you some feedback.

Make sure you have no spelling mistakes in your video or content - it will increase your chances. There are at least two in the video.
The list of all marketing agencies is super valuable and a good showcase of potential skills
But I am still not sure exactly what work and skills you are offering….its in the middle of the email, but you should highlight this earlier.

So, in the interests of doing a small tes, I will give you a scenario and see what you come back with.

Our focus as an agency is to get more work with the various different organisations that are part of the NSW Government. There are many departments which cover different areas (Customer Services, Health, University of NSW, etc etc.)

Come back with a recommendation on how we can target the people who work in these organisations who would want to use our marketing agency for their marketing projects.
Come back with the type of data you might be able to supply.
Find out how NSW Govt run tenders for marketing projects.

There is no urgency with this. Just looking to see what you can do.

Also open to hearing what else you think you may be able to do to help our agency - maybe its about helping our Google My Business ranking, maybe its about building great "ChatGPT" templates for specific marketing tasks. Ultimately you're showing us data, but they need to be applied to how we may be able to benefit our clients.

Thanks,
Geoff.
